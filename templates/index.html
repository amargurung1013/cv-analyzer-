About

What this is

CV reads resumes the way a recruiter would, then writes up the kind of report a recruiter would hand to a hiring manager.

01

Upload

Drop in one resume or a whole stack of PDFs at once. Each one is read and analyzed on its own.

02

Analyze

An AI reviewer reads each resume for ATS compatibility, strengths, weaknesses, and overall career level.

03

Score

Every resume gets a 0–100 score, so a batch of candidates can be compared at a glance.

04

Report

Results land on a per-candidate report page, one resume per page, ranked highest score first.
